About Artificial intelligence

Artificial intelligence (AI) is a set of technologies that enable computers to perform a variety of advanced functions, including the ability to see, understand and translate spoken and written language, analyze data, make recommendations, and more.

About cyber security

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting people, systems and data from cyberattacks by using various technologies, processes and policies.At the enterprise level, cybersecurity is key to overall risk management strategy, and specifically, cyber risk management.

About cloud computing

Cloud computing is a way to access and use IT resources—like servers, storage, software, and analytics—over the internet on an on-demand, pay-as-you-go basis. Instead of managing their own physical hardware and infrastructure, organizations and individuals access these services from remote data centers owned by cloud providers.
